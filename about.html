About Us

Mecca Care is a Philadelphia based community home care service provider. Our clients are family and family comes first. We are dedicated to providing quality non medical health care to patients living with physical and/or medical disabilities and their families. We assist people in maintaining the ability to remain independent in their own home. Our services reduce and /or eliminate the need for hospitalization or other institutional care by providing exceptional care at home via a family member, friend or high qualified Mecca Care LLC personal assistance caregiver. What sets us apart is the saying “Our clients are family and family comes first” is not just a slogan. It is something we embody as the backbone of our company. It represents the way we treat every consumer and hopefully the way they feel about us. Personal care attendant services can be given by a family members, friends, or by one of our highly trained home health aides. Our attendants help disabled, chronically ill and cognitively challenged clients with personal care needs within the comfort of their own homes. These services include assistance with daily living as needed, self- administration of medication, grooming, bathing, housekeeping, laundry, addressing patient concerns and requests, help with meals, and observation and reporting.
